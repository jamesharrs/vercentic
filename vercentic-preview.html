The AI Platform as a Service for Human Capital Management.

Vercentic isn't just software you configure — it's infrastructure you build on. A fully programmable, AI-native platform that gives talent teams, RPOs, and enterprise HR the foundation to create exactly the system they need — without writing a line of code.

Multi-Tenant Architecture
Provision isolated client environments in minutes. Each tenant gets their own data model, workflows, branding, and user base — completely separated, centrally managed.
AI-Native Core
Intelligence isn't a feature — it's the foundation. Matching, copilot, document parsing, translation, and JD generation all run on the same AI layer, embedded in every workflow.
Configurable Data Model
Create any object — People, Jobs, Clients, Projects — with 16+ field types, conditional visibility, and relationships. Your schema, not ours.
API-First Platform

White-Label Portals

Solutions

Built for the teams doing the most complex talent work.

Whether you're running an in-house function at a global enterprise or managing recruitment delivery for multiple clients, Vercentic gives you the intelligence, control, and configurability to operate at a different level.

Enterprise talent acquisition

Run global hiring from one command centre.

Enterprise TA teams face a uniquely complex challenge — multiple business units, geographies, hiring managers, and compliance frameworks, often with legacy tooling stitched together with spreadsheets. Vercentic replaces the patchwork with a single, fully configurable platform that adapts to your structure, not the other way around.

  • Multi-entity and multi-environment support — configure separately per region or brand
  • Role-based access so hiring managers, interviewers, and business partners see only what's relevant to them
  • AI matching surfaces the right internal and external candidates against every role
  • Real-time pipeline reporting with dashboards built around your KPIs, not generic metrics
  • Org structure management — visualise headcount, open roles, and succession in one view
  • Branded candidate and hiring manager portals without a separate vendor
RPO & talent outsourcing

Deliver recruitment as a premium service.

RPOs and managed service providers are judged on speed, quality, and the experience they create for clients and candidates. Vercentic gives you the platform infrastructure to deliver all three — with client-branded environments, real-time visibility, and AI that works in the background so your team can focus on the relationships that matter.
